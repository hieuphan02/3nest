Our AI Service

The Future of Unified Management and Intelligent Control

3nest's Cloud AIoT platform is designed for large enterprises, offering robust cloud management capabilities that seamlessly integrate intelligent monitoring, IoT devices, AI analytics, and access control systems. Through this platform, businesses can achieve comprehensive data management, automate process optimization, and enhance security measures.

Core Features

Cloud Video Surveillance

A cloud-based video monitoring system that supports real-time video streaming, remote viewing, and historical recording storage, ensuring the safety of critical areas around the clock.

IoT Interactive Control

The platform can unify management and automatically control various IoT devices, including access control systems, sensors, and alarm devices, enabling intelligent operations and device interactivity.

AI Object and Facial Recognition

Built-in AI facial recognition functionality supports personnel authentication, quickly identifying and recording entrants and enhancing security levels.

IoT Sensor Integration

Capable of connecting to various types of IoT sensors (temperature, humidity, vibration, light, etc.) for comprehensive environmental monitoring, real-time anomaly detection, and triggering alarms.

Enterprise Access Control

Supports intelligent access control systems, providing multiple identity authentication methods such as NFC cards and wristbands for efficient access management.

NFC Card and Wristband Tracking

Utilizes NFC technology for real-time location tracking of personnel and equipment, enhancing internal safety and management efficiency.

Temperature, Humidity Control and Alerts

The platform continuously monitors and controls the temperature and humidity within the enterprise. It triggers immediate alerts when thresholds are exceeded, ensuring safe equipment operation.

AI Algorithm Analysis

Employs AI algorithms to analyze various collected data, providing real-time reports, predicting equipment failures, and offering operational optimization suggestions.

Platform Advantages

ISO 27001 Security Certification

The platform complies with international information security management standards, ensuring the security of enterprise data and equipment against cyber threats and attacks.

Centralized Management

Through a unified management interface, businesses can remotely control devices and data across multiple branches, achieving centralized management and operations across regions.

Flexible Scalability

Regardless of the enterprise size, the platform can flexibly scale to support more devices and larger-scale data processing, meeting future growth needs.

Real-time Data Analysis and Reporting

The platform provides real-time data analysis and automated report generation features, helping businesses make swift decisions.

Multi-level User Permission Management

Enterprises can set different operational permissions for employees at various levels, ensuring secure system usage.

Rapid Fault Detection and Repair

The built-in AI analysis model can instantly detect faults and provide preventive maintenance suggestions, reducing equipment failure rates.
