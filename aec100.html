3nest Edge Computing Device

AEC100 Series

The 3nest Edge Computer (AEC) 100 series operates at the network edge, empowering AI and IoT devices. It offers seamless connectivity, advanced analytics, efficient management, and software-defined AI for processing video and data. Enterprises can quickly integrate with AtomCloud and leverage the combined benefits of AIoT, edge computing, and cloud infrastructure.

Cloud Auto Provisioning

Hassle-free large-scale deployment. The device automatically retrieves configuration from the cloud, simplifying multi-site deployment and making it quick and easy.

Multi-Layer Visual AI Algorithm

Enhance discoverability with multi-layer visual AI. Extract valuable metadata from video content using software-defined AI, enabling more precise content analysis.

Video and Audio Analysis & Cloud Recording

AEC manages video and audio streams, providing the first layer of AI processing. It leverages TCP/IP and Onvif standards to collect video and audio sources, enabling AI analysis at the edge. Quickly integrates with existing infrastructure for seamless cloud-based recording and AI operations.

Extend to IoT Edge

Enable wired or wireless IoT connectivity at the edge through TCP/IP, RS232/485, Zwave, Zigbee, or other USB-enabled methods. AEC collects and manages IoT data, analyzes it, and triggers actions using software-defined AI in the cloud, ensuring smooth coordination across enterprise networks.

AEC100 Edge AI Features

Motion AI

Dynamic Motion Detection

A powerful tool to detect object changes within seconds, ensuring real-time response.

Audio AI

Sound-Activated Recording

Trigger event recording automatically based on detected sounds.

Software-Defined AI Sound Matching

The edge device responds to specific sound waves, activating audio/video recording or IoT actions automatically.

Facial AI

Face Detection

Detect, track, and store captured faces for future use.

Face Categorization and Search

Extract facial features and organize them into a layered database, enabling fast and accurate searches.

Face Match

Perform real-time comparison between captured faces and cloud-stored facial data, providing confidence scores and historical appearance logs.

Object AI

Smart Parking

Classify vehicles such as cars, motorcycles, and bicycles in designated areas and provide real-time status updates.

Occupancy AI

Identify and classify people within marked areas, providing real-time occupancy status for cloud AI integration.

Object Detection and Classification

Detect and classify objects using up to 100 pre-trained AI models, enabling precise recognition and analysis.
