About 3nest

Home Company

The senior management team at 3nest consists of Silicon Valley veterans with experience at companies such as Cisco, Yahoo, and HP. They have been involved in startups from inception to exit, including IPOs and mergers, and have experience with trading on NASDAQ and NYSE. The founders have strong backgrounds in technology and finance, with degrees from prestigious academic institutions such as Cornell University and USC. With over 50 years of industry experience, we extend our value to team members and provide the best experience to our customers.

3nest inherits the spirit of Silicon Valley and is based in the beautiful city of Issaquah, near Seattle. This location is surrounded by high-tech leaders like Microsoft and Amazon.

About 3nest

Welcome to 3nest, where cutting-edge technology meets visionary innovation. Since our inception in 2016, we have been at the forefront of the technological revolution, combining the power of artificial intelligence (AI) with the limitless potential of cloud and edge computing infrastructure. Our company was founded by seasoned veterans of Silicon Valley, individuals who have achieved remarkable success in previous start-ups, taking them all the way to IPO.

At 3nest, we embody a culture of technical innovation that runs deep within our DNA. We believe in pushing boundaries and reshaping the landscape of what is possible. Our team of brilliant minds is dedicated to exploring the uncharted territories of AI, harnessing its transformative capabilities to create solutions that shape the future.

Our primary objective is to bring value to our customers and the broader technology ecosystem. We are driven by the idea that technology can be a force for good, empowering individuals, businesses, and industries to achieve new heights of success. Through our AI-driven solutions and cutting-edge infrastructure, we aim to revolutionize the way businesses operate and people experience the world.

Our commitment to innovation is matched by our dedication to excellence. We strive for nothing less than the highest standards in every aspect of our work. From research and development to implementation and support, we ensure that every product and service we deliver meets and exceeds the expectations of our customers.

We understand that the true value of technology lies in its ability to solve real-world problems. That's why we work closely with our clients, forging strong partnerships that enable us to deeply understand their needs and tailor our solutions to address their unique challenges. By listening, collaborating, and co-creating, we build long-lasting relationships that drive mutual success.

In an ever-evolving digital landscape, we recognize the importance of adaptability. We stay ahead of the curve, constantly evolving our technology stack and refining our expertise to meet the demands of a rapidly changing world. By leveraging the latest advancements in AI, cloud computing, and edge infrastructure, we empower our customers to navigate the complexities of the digital age with confidence.

We are more than just a tech company; we are a community of dreamers, thinkers, and problem solvers. Our diverse team, comprising top talent from around the globe, collaborates seamlessly to bring forth innovative solutions that make a tangible difference. We foster an inclusive and empowering work environment that values creativity, collaboration, and the pursuit of excellence.

Join 3nest and our ecosystem on this transformative journey as we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible. Together, we can unlock the full potential of AI and technology to shape a better future for all.
